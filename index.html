About Us

MockTank is a youth-led virtual startup pitch competition where teens can unleash their entrepreneurial spirit. Whether you're a high schooler with a bold idea or a curious problem-solver, this platform is for you.

From ideation to final pitch, MockTank equips participants with mentorship, confidence, and presentation skills â€” all in a supportive, student-run environment. No pressure, just possibility.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Register Online
    Apply solo or as a team of up to 3.
  2. Step 2: Submit Your Business Idea
    Teams submit a short written proposal outlining their startup idea.
  3. Step 3: Semi-Finalist Selection
    Judges review submissions and select semi-finalists.
  4. Step 4: Mentorship + Pitch Prep
    Semi-finalists attend a virtual mentorship session.
  5. Step 5: Live Semi-Final Pitch
    Teams present their pitch live on Zoom to judges.
  6. Step 6: Finalist Announcement
    Top teams are selected for the final round.
  7. Step 7: Final Pitch + Winner Selection
    Finalists compete for awards. Winners announced.
